Ponies!

What are ponies? Ponies are small horses. They are very cute and have many different colors. Some people like to collect them, and some people like to ride them. Ponies are very friendly animals, and they are often used in therapy for children and adults.

If you want to learn more about ponies, there are many websites and books about them. You can also find out about ponies by going to a pony show or riding a pony yourself.
